dungeon rules & etiquettes

To ensure a safe, enjoyable, and respectful experience, all guests must follow these guidelines.

General Rules

  • Consent is Mandatory: All activities must be agreed upon by all parties involved. No means no, and boundaries must always be respected.
  • Respect the Space & Equipment: Treat all furniture, tools, and dungeon accessories with care. Clean any equipment after use.
  • No Photography or Recording: Privacy is our top priority. No cameras, phones, or recording devices are allowed in play areas.
  • Arrive on Time: Sessions run on a strict schedule. Late arrivals may result in reduced session time.
  • Dress Code: Adhere to the venue's dress code. Street clothes are allowed in reception areas but should be changed before entering play zones.

Behavioral Etiquette

  • No Interruptions: Do not interrupt ongoing scenes or engage with others unless invited.
  • Use Safe Words: Always establish and respect safe words to ensure a safe and consensual experience.
  • Maintain Hygiene: Good personal hygiene is expected. Shower before a session and use deodorant.
  • No Drugs or Alcohol: Intoxication is strictly prohibited. Anyone under the influence will be denied entry.
  • Follow Dungeon Monitor Instructions: Our Dungeon Monitors ensure a safe environment. Always follow their guidance.

Aftercare & Closing Guidelines

  • Respect Personal Space: Aftercare is important. Allow others to have their time to recover and process.
  • Clean Up After Yourself: Wipe down surfaces, return equipment, and leave the space as you found it.
  • Report Issues: If you see or experience any violations, report them to the staff immediately.

We are committed to providing a secure and welcoming environment where everyone can explore their desires freely and respectfully.
